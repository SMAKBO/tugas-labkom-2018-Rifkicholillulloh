Physical properties include:

The colorless liquid with a boiling point of 64.5 ° C, can be mixed with water in any comparison, not forming an azeotropic mixture with water. More toxic than ethylalcohol. Soluble with water, with alcohol, with ether, benzene, and with most other organic solvents.

Here are some physical properties of menthol:

Material Quality

Mentol compounds are included in the class of cyclic monoterpene derivatives. Chemical compounds in this class have characteristics that contain two double bonds and one circle. Examples of compounds in this class besides menthol are menton, terpinol, terpienol. Mentol is present in peppermint oil and synthesized by a method of hydrogenation of thymol. The crystalline solids in the form of menthol granules will melt at 45 ° C.

"The properties of menthol are slightly soluble in water, but they are readily soluble in alcohol, chloroform, and ether. Mentol has properties as an antiseptic that can inhibit germs and analgesics. Menthol is also commonly used in cold medication. Menthol compounds are also classified as irritant-sensitive compounds with a cooling sensation at concentrations of 1.25% to 16%. "
