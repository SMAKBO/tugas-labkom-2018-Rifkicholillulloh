Side effects of menthol

Like all other medicines, menthol also has some side effects, of which some are mild, while others might require immediate medical attention. Common dangers or side effects that some people have experienced while using menthol


Menthol nasal area apply is used to offer temporary relief from congested nose, coughing and a sore throat due to cold, allergies, sinus problems, or other breathing infections. Despite its effects, many people have experience some side effects after using the nasal spray. This includes difficulty in breathing, difficulty swallowing, chest hardness, discomfort in the nasal area, soreness and discomfort at site of application, etc.

Various creams and lotions made by Menthol crystals are generally used by people who experience continuous muscle pain or pain due to joint disease or any other condition. However, some of them have noticed some serious allergies like skin allergy, itching, soreness and discomfort at the application area and cities. Other unusual but serious menthol cream adverse responses include respiration problems, chest area hardness, inflammation of the mouth, lips, face, tongue, etc.
